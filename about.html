WHO WE ARE

About Uhambe Memorial Commercial College

Uhambe Memorial Commercial College Taraku is dedicated to providing quality secondary education in a disciplined, supportive and inspiring learning environment.

We strive to develop students academically, morally and socially while preparing them for higher education, leadership and responsible citizenship.

OUR HISTORY

School History

Uhambe Memorial Commercial College Taraku was established with the vision of providing quality education and nurturing future leaders. As a growing institution, the school remains committed to academic excellence, discipline and moral values.
